    About Us

    In-Home, One-on-One Personal Training

    Busy work schedules and family life may undermine your ability to make it into the gym or other workout facilities to exercise. Not anymore! Weights On Wheels, LLC is fully insured and objective is to assist you in reaching your personal fitness goals while conveniently working inside your own home, making it easier for you to get a sufficient workout even on the busiest days. The intent is for us to travel to you. Whether your favorite place to work out is in your own home, your own backyard, or a local park, we will supply all of the equipment, meet you at your favorite spot to exercise, and provide you with a quality workout that is personalized for your specific goals and fitness needs.

    In addition to providing clients with personalized workouts, We also offer meal plans that will change the way you look at “healthy food” forever. Weights On Wheels, LLC offers convenient, one-hour, in-home personal training sessions for a cost that is much more affordable than a gym membership. Personal trainers at a gym can cost a fortune; We are not only charging less than a typical personal trainer, but we are willing to train you in the comfort of your own home.

    Therapeutic Stretching

    Weights On Wheels, LLC offers therapeutic stretching, focusing on range of motion for your muscles and joints so that they don’t get too sore after working them. Not many understand the importance of pre- and post-workout stretches. These stretches are performed to help prepare and relax the muscles before and after working them out for an elongated period of time. Before and after EVERY one-on- one training session, we will ensure that your body is properly stretched and rehabilitated.

    Muscle Building/Muscle Toning

    Weights On Wheels, LLC provides the weights and workout plans necessary to help build and tone your muscles; all you have to do is sign up! Building muscles and toning muscles are two of my specialties. Combining our specialized workout plans with our meal preparation is a recipe for greatness. Join today and see for yourself!

    Body Maintenance

    There is a common misconception that maintaining a healthy physique takes extensive time and extreme effort. However, with Weights On Wheels’ personalized workout plans, you can stay in shape and easily maintain your body with as few as three workouts per week!

    Weight Loss

    Weight loss is one of the many reasons that people search for a personal trainer. Search no more! Weights On Wheels, LLC focuses on your needs and tailors workout plans to meet your specific fitness goals. With our personalized workout plans, we will work with weights, perform resistance exercises, and will also teach you countless other routines that will help you reach your ultimate weight loss goals. We personally guarantee that our clients will see results within the first month. Our methods have been proven to be successful; sign up today and become the next success story!

    Meal Preparation

    The key to a healthy body and a healthy lifestyle is dieting. In addition to the personalized workout plans and training that we offer, we also offer meal plans to go along with your workout routines. Building a healthy physique starts in the kitchen! We can work out all we want, but the real results happen when we get on the right diet plan. Too busy to cook food everyday yourself? Our weekly meal preparation plans take just one hour of your day to make your meals for the entire work week.
